# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We want everyone to feel welcome and respected in this project.

We expect all contributors, maintainers, and users to follow this Code of Conduct in all project spaces, including issues and pull requests.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and seek to understand
- Provide clear and actionable feedback
- Acknowledge and credit others when appropriate

## Unacceptable behavior
Examples include:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Threats or abusive language
- Troll behavior or repeated disruptive activity
- Posting private or sensitive information
- Any form of retaliation against people who reported issues in good faith

## Enforcement
If you believe someone has violated this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers.

Maintainership may remove issues/PRs, restrict access, or take other actions they deem appropriate to protect the community.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is inspired by common community standards for open-source projects.
